TRASH MANAGEMENT

As we know that trash management is the most essential part in our day-to-day life. For personal hygiene also it plays a very important role and sanitation itself. In Japan government is trying to do its best for the proper management of trash. For international students while coming to Japan, its quite confusing. In Japan, each municipality has their own rules for the separation of the garbage. We have to keep them in separate plastic. We have to keep them in garbage collecting place near home so every twice a week or once according to municipality schedule they will collect the garbage. Simply garbage in divided in four major categories. They are as follows: -

  • Burnable Garbage |   kitchen waste outcome, paper scrabs, small cloth etc.
  • Non- Burnable Garbage |   metal product, glass, pottery etc.
  • Recyclable Garbage |   newspaper, container jar, can, plastic bottles etc.
  • Oversized Garbage |   : large furniture, fridge, washing machine etc.

  • AEON

When talking about the cheap stores in Aeon we can find DAISO (100 yen) shop. Many grocessary items and fast food like Sashimi are at discounted prices here.
