ICOCA CARD

An ICOCA cards are rechargeable smart cards which can be used to ride JR west trains or busses without buying tickets by touching the electric ticket gate or electric ticket machine and you can pay money in a shop around city displaying ICOCA marks. It is an electric card which helps people to buy tickets easily and saves time and it reduces lots of pressure of getting changes in japan.
